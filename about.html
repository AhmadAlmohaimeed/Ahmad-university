About Us

We are the world's largest University

King Saud bin Abdulaziz University for Health Sciences (KSAU-HS) is a governmental university specialized in health sciences. KSAU-HS is accredited by the Ministry of Education in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia for its various programs for undergraduate and postgraduate degrees.
These academic programs at King Abdulaziz Medical City, which are part of the Ministry of National Guard Health Affairs, represented its first nucleus. With the fast growth of these programs and their positive results in preparing Saudi cadres of Saudi cadres in Medical fields and health sciences, the Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques, King Abdullah bin Abdulaziz issued a royal decree on 5 Safar 1426 corresponding to 6 March 2005, to establish the university named “King Saud bin Abdulaziz University for Health Sciences", based in Riyadh and has two campuses in Jeddah and Al-Ahsa. KSAU-HS has 14 colleges in three university campuses in Riyadh, Jeddah, and Al-Ahsa:

• Seven colleges in the university campus in Riyadh
College of Medicine
College of Dentistry
College of Pharmacy
College of Public Health and Health Informatics
College of Applied Medical Sciences
College of Nursing
College of Sciences and Health Professions

• Four colleges in the university campus in Jeddah
College of Medicine
College of Applied Medical Sciences
College of Nursing
College of Health Sciences and Health Professions

• Three colleges in the university campus in Al-Ahsa
College of Applied Medical Sciences
College of Nursing
College of Sciences and Health Professions
